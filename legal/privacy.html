Privacy Policy

Ludi · Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ludi ("we", "us", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Ludi mobile application and related services. By using Ludi, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to operate the app:

  • Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address. If you use Sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the identifier and email those services provide.
  • Profile data: Your chosen nickname and avatar.
  • Learning progress: Your XP, lives, streaks, league level, lesson completion, practice results, and achievements.
  • Subscription status: Whether you hold an active Ludi Pro subscription (processed by the App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat — we never see your card details).
  • Technical data: Basic device and diagnostic information used to keep the app stable.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To save and sync your learning progress across your devices.
  • To display leaderboards and community features.
  • To provide and manage Ludi Pro subscription features.
  • To improve the app's content and reliability.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted providers to deliver the app:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Cloud Firestore) — account and progress storage.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing.

Each provider handles your data under its own privacy policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers and protected by security rules that restrict access to your own account. We take reasonable measures to safeguard your information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Contact below).

6. Children's Privacy

Ludi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data, and withdraw consent where applicable. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests, contact us at: support@ludi.app
